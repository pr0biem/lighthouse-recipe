Peanut Butter Butterscotch Marshmallow Squares

Ingredients
  • ½ Cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 300g Butterscotch Chips
  • 200g Mini-Marshmallows

Directions
  • In a saucepan melt the butter and mix in your peanut butter and butterscotch chips.
    		•
  • Once nicely melted, allow mixture to cool.
    		•
  • Once the mixture is cool enough not to melt them, pour the mixture into a bowl with the mini marshmallows.
    		•
  • Stir everything together.
    		•
  • Grease an 11" x 13" pan and pour the mixture in.
    		•
  • Refridgerate the mixture. Once cold, cut the bars as desired.
    		•
